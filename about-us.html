Getting to know Barboni's

Barboni's is a family run restaurant established in 2014 and located in Omagh, County Tyrone. We offer a wide variety of food to try, from traditional American and Italian favourites to the more adventurous spiced blends of Mexico.

We are a small intimate, licensed premises offering a selection of wines, spirits, beers and other alcoholic beverages. Our informal environment creates the ideal venue for a great night out with friends or family.

So if you like the sound of good food, nice wine, cool music and a welcoming smile, why not drop in and try out the Barboni's experience.
